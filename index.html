About

IEC Group of Institutions is one of India's premier educational groups with courses in the field of Engineering and Pharmacy. A lot has changed in IEC ever since it embarked on this journey nearly 3 decades ago except the Group's unflinching commitment to provide quality education to its students. We are committed towards elite undergraduate and postgraduate training and education which is based on excellence in academic and applied research. The mission is to enhance knowledge and train students in the field of science and technology and other areas. IEC Group of Institutions currently has strength of more than 3000 students.

We are pleased to inform you that IEC Group of Institutions is going to organize its Annual Sports and Cultural Fest INNOVISION 2018 in its campus on 21 ST to 22 nd March 2018. The three days extravaganza includes mixture of different sporting events. Students from different institutions are invited for the events. It will be our pleasure to have you as a part of “INNOVISION 2018”. You are requested to spread publicity in your Institute, motivate your students to take part in maximum activities in this event.
